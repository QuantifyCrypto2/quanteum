The Quanteum Project provides a platform of tools and information for participants to become a proficient crypto-currency trader.

This is achieved through state of the art quantitative algorithms, alerts, tools and analysis for the advanced, intermediate and beginner crypto-currency trader. Traders will be able to select from established algorithms provided by Quanteum, use a public algorithm designed by another Quant Credits trader, or create a custom-made algorithm using our software tool library designed for a non-programmer. Understanding how the crypto market behaves and researching the various alt-coins available can be time-consuming, especially for a novice investor. Our platform eliminates the barrier to digital currency information by offering the trader key facts and figures needed to make savvy decisions in the crypto-currency arena. This will include coin summary descriptions, coin news and chatrooms and blogs of fellow traders. The user will have the ability to select coins of interest to receive real time pricing. Additionally, the user will be able to customize notifications received via email, SMS and/or the Quant Credits app. The notifications can include buy/sell alerts from the algorithms, regular updates of the price of one or more coin(s), or when a “hot topic” is being discussed in the chatroom or blog. The beginner trader will have the ability to receive support for the algorithms or coin combinations they desire..

FAQ

What is Quant Credits?

Quanteum (symbol: EQL) is a ERC20-compliant token based on the Ethereum blockchain technology. Quanteum EQL is the core asset in the new Quanteum Ecosystem. Users possessing Quanteum EQL can exchange it for digital services or physical goods.

What does the Quant Credit Token represent?

The Quant Credit Token is used to obtain services and information available on the Quant Credit Website. They are not securities or for speculation. There is no promise of future performance. There is no suggestion or promise that Quant Credits has or will hold a particular value. Quant Credits gives no rights in the company and do not represent participation in the company. Quanteum EQL is sold as a functional good. Any value received by the company may be spent without conditions. Quanteum Initial Coin Offering purchases are meant only for experts in cryptographic tokens and blockchain - based software systems. There is no guarantee that Quanteum or the Quanteum commission rewards and micro-payment platform will be successful and have any long-term value whatsoever.

What amount is being raised? What’s the cap of tokens? Will there be a follow-on offering?

We are targeting a raise of as much as $18 million USD and a cap of one billion Quanteum tokens. We do not plan to have a follow-on offering.

What crypto-currencies are accepted in the crowd-sale?

ETH will be accepted in the crowdsale. You will be required to have an Ethereum wallet pointed at the token/crowdsale address to participate in the crowdsale. Quanteum is an Ethereum derived token. If you hold BTC or another crypto-currency it can be exchanged for ETH via third-parties and used to participate in the crowdsale.

When will the crowdsale happen?

The public crowdsale is schedule to occur in March 2018. The exact day will be dependent on finalizing the contract and completing the full audit, which may be changed by Quanteum LLC at a later time. The contract will be made available on the Quanteum Official public Github repository once it is finalized.

What is the price of Quant Tokens?

Quant Credits will be sold at a fixed ratio to ETH. The goal is to raise up to $18 million USD equivalent in ETH for selling 1 Billion Quanteum Tokens, which will determine the exchange rate, and will be set immediately before deploying the contract.

How will Quant Credits through SparkCo use ETH raised during token launch?

The ETH received in the crowdsale will be used by SparkCo to improve our agent reward ecosystem, build out a micro-payment system, and onboard additional digital partners as explained in this document.
